What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is a common lung infection caused by bacteria, a virus or fungi. It is often spread via coughing, sneezing, touching or even breathing, and those who don't exhibit symptoms can also spread the illness. Learn more about Pneumonia

-From the American Lung Association.
